Methodology

Academic Preparation Pipeline

Five structured phases engineered to align directly with university deliverables and calendar due dates.

01

Share Your Requirement

Provide your university specialization (NMIMS, WeSchool, Symbiosis, etc.), target project topics, guidelines, and outline specifications. If you don't have a topic, we will brainstorm options together.

02

Discuss Project Details

Align on target chapter listings, bibliography methods, PPT page totals, and deadline schedules. We ensure every syllabus requirement is fully captured before starting.

03

Project Preparation

Deep content research and assembly. We craft the full academic report, undertaking declarations, index columns, guide certificate listings, and highly structured presentation slides.

04

Review & Delivery

Initial draft submission for your validation. If you need any structural updates, table changes, or paragraph alignment modifications, we compile revisions promptly.

05

Submission Support

Receive your submission-ready final DOCX/PPT/PDF packages. We provide final guidance to prepare you for your online LMS portal upload and viva defense preparation details.

FAQ Portal

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to key logistics, customization rules, timeline schedules, and formatting questions.

What types of MBA projects do you support?

+
We provide structured academic preparation and formatting support across Marketing, Finance, HR, Operations, Information Technology, and Supply Chain specializations. We align strictly to Hybrid, Online, Part-Time, and Distance learning curriculum standards for universities like WeSchool (Welingkar), NMIMS, Symbiosis, Amity, IGNOU, and others.

How long does delivery take?

+
Standard delivery for First Year Projects takes between 3 to 5 business days. Extensive Second Year Projects (including full academic research reports and PowerPoint slides) typically take 7 to 10 business days. Priority turnaround options are available upon special request during peak university schedules.

Can projects be customized?

+
Yes, absolutely. 100% of our support is customized to your selected topic and university requirements. We sit down to gather your industry constraints, case studies, or specific company details to construct a unique, personalized thesis aligned with exact guidelines.

Do you provide PPTs along with reports?

+
Yes, Second Year MBA packages explicitly bundle a comprehensive 55-65 page project report alongside a beautifully structured 18-20 slide PowerPoint presentation deck designed for your final academic viva defense.

How do I place an order?

+
Simply fill out the inquiry form at the bottom of the page or click the sticky WhatsApp button to chat directly. We will review your university handbook guidelines, agree on topic details and packaging tiers, and initiate research immediately upon plan approval.

Can working professionals get assistance?

+
Yes, the vast majority of our students are working professionals between 25-40 years old who balance intensive leadership roles, families, and academic classes. Our system is fully designed to deliver submission-ready materials with minimal overhead for you.

What information do I need to provide?

+
Ideally, share your university academic project handbook, exact topic preferences (if selected), submission calendar deadlines, and any specific templates or cover worksheets provided by your guide. If you do not have a topic, we will brainstorm specialized themes together.
